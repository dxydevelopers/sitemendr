# Homepage - Call-to-Action Section

## Overview
The final CTA section creates urgency and drives conversions by offering a limited-time incentive and clear next steps. It should compel visitors to take immediate action.

## Layout Structure
- **Centered content block** with compelling headline
- **Value proposition** with time-sensitive offer
- **Dual CTA buttons** for different user intents
- **Trust indicators** and social proof
- **Background design** that creates visual impact

## Content Elements

### Main Headline (H2)
**Text:** "Ready to Transform Your Online Presence?"
**Styling:** 42px font-size, white text, bold weight
**Animation:** Fade in with slight upward movement

### Subheadline
**Text:** "Join 500+ businesses who've launched successful websites with Sitemendr"
**Styling:** 20px font-size, light gray text
**Animation:** Fade in after headline

### Value Proposition
**Text:** "🎯 Free AI Website Assessment (Value $299)
⏰ Limited Time: Next 50 clients get complimentary strategy session
💰 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee"
**Styling:** Bullet points with icons, 18px font-size
**Animation:** Staggered fade-in for each point

### Primary CTA Button
**Text:** "Get Free Assessment Now"
**Styling:** Large blue gradient button, 20px padding, white text
**Hover Effect:** Brightness increase, scale up, glow effect
**Action:** Open AI Assessment modal or redirect to assessment page

### Secondary CTA Button
**Text:** "Schedule Consultation"
**Styling:** Outline button with blue border, transparent background
**Hover Effect:** Fill with blue background
**Action:** Open calendar booking modal
