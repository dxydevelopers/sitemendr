# Homepage - Hero Section

## Overview
The hero section is the first impression visitors get of Sitemendr. It must immediately communicate our value proposition: "AI-powered web development with human expertise."

## Layout Structure
- **Full-width hero** with background video/image
- **Centered content block** with headline, subheadline, CTA buttons
- **Trust indicators** below the fold
- **Scroll indicator** at bottom

## Content Elements

### Headline (H1)
**Text:** "AI-Powered Websites, Human Expertise"
**Styling:** 48px font-size, gradient text (blue to purple), bold weight
**Animation:** Typewriter effect on load

### Subheadline (H2)
**Text:** "From concept to launch in weeks, not months. Modern websites that drive real business results."
**Styling:** 24px font-size, light gray color, line-height 1.4
**Animation:** Fade in after headline completes

### Primary CTA Button
**Text:** "Get AI Website Assessment"
**Styling:** Blue gradient background, white text, 16px padding, rounded corners
**Hover Effect:** Brightness increase, slight scale up
**Action:** Scroll to AI Assessment section or open modal

### Secondary CTA Button
**Text:** "View Our Work"
**Styling:** Transparent background, blue border, blue text
**Hover Effect:** Background fill with blue
**Action:** Smooth scroll to portfolio section

### Trust Indicators
**Element 1:** "500+ Websites Launched"
**Element 2:** "99.9% Uptime Guarantee"
**Element 3:** "24/7 Expert Support"
**Element 4:** "30-Day Money Back"

## Background
- **Type:** Animated gradient background
- **Colors:** Deep blue to purple gradient
- **Animation:** Subtle particle effect or geometric shapes floating
- **Overlay:** Dark overlay for text readability

## Mobile Responsiveness
- **Headline:** 32px on mobile
- **Subheadline:** 18px on mobile
- **Buttons:** Stacked vertically on mobile
- **Padding:** Reduced margins on mobile

## Interactions
- **Scroll Trigger:** Elements animate in on scroll
- **Hover States:** Button animations
- **Click Tracking:** Analytics for CTA performance

## Accessibility
- **Alt Text:** For any images used
- **Keyboard Navigation:** Tab order for buttons
- **Screen Reader:** Proper heading hierarchy
- **Color Contrast:** WCAG AA compliant

## Performance
- **Lazy Loading:** Background assets load after hero text
- **Optimized Images:** WebP format with fallbacks
- **Minimal JS:** Only essential animations

## A/B Testing Variables
- **Headline variations:** Test different value propositions
- **CTA text:** Test "Start Free Assessment" vs "Get AI Assessment"
- **Trust indicators:** Test different social proof elements
